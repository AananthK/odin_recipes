Brownies

Description

Brownies are an indulgent dessert, perfect for chocolate lovers. Theres not many treats that can top a fudgy, gooey, dense brownie.

Brownies primarity focus on incorporating chocate in a chocolate-cake-like mixture with a different ratio or flour, eggs, sugar and butter. This recipe provides a simple method to make these delectable squares with a nutty twist!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined. Fold in chopped walnuts. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes; cool before slicing into squares.
